Bio

Discount Fireworks is an exercise in auditory interdependence. Jeff Maleri (bass) and Ben Myers (drums) resuscitate and evolve melodies that have long haunted Joe Sepi. Sepi offers heart on his sleeve vocals and a signature post-punk, no wave guitar sensibility. Maleri's bass stylings are soulful, blues-infused and entrancing. Myers' post-rock pounding percussion tethers his bandmates. What emerges is a trio that effortlessly travels time signatures and invites negative space. What emerges is a kind of cerebral rock that feels both familiar and new.

Members have shared bills with Mission of Burma, Modest Mouse, Deerhoof, Built to Spill, Richard Lloyd, Grant Hart, The Thermals, Pile, Speedy Ortiz, SAVAK and more...

RIYL Fugazi, Husker Du, Parquet Courts, Pere Ubu, Guided By Voices, Wipers, Built to Spill, Kinks, Flaming Lips, Les Savy Fav, PUP, Pavement, Wire, KARP, Naked Raygun, Dinosaur Jr, Metz, Sonic Youth, Danielson, Spoon, Hot Snakes, Gang of Four, Cardiacs

Blurb

Discount Fireworks is an exercise in auditory interdependence. Entrancing, blues-infused bass marries inventive and pounding drums that elevate and evolve heart-on-your-sleeve vocal melodies, punctuated by post-punk, no wave guitars. What emerges is a kind of humble, cerebral rock trio that effortlessly travels time signatures and invites negative space.
